# Cover Email Writing Skill

A good application email should:

1. Address the hiring team or recruiter politely.
2. State the role clearly.
3. Mention the attached tailored CV.
4. Summarize fit in 1-2 sentences.
5. Keep the body concise.
6. Avoid exaggerated claims.
7. Avoid unresolved placeholders.

The email must always be shown to the user for review before sending.
